Photoshops of Jackson's Face on Things

Jackson Czarnecki is a long time punching bag of the friend group. He once sent out a picture of his face, and I immediately downloaded it. I thought, what could I do with this picture? And then, I found the answer. I created a discord server that has one purpose in mind. Make. Fun. Of. Jackson. I invited my friends to this server (including Jackson) and we started to tinker with these pictures. About a half a year later, I was learning CSS, and thought, why don't I make a website for this? And that is the website you're seeing now. All of these pictures are beautiful photoshops of Jackson at his finest, and it was the perfect project for me. Good luck getting these pictures out of your head.
